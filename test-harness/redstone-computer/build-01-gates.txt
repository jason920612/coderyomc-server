# coderyoMC redstone-computer MAP — rung-1 logic gates (server console commands)
# Comparator convention: FACING points at the REAR/INPUT; output exits the opposite side.
# SUBTRACT comparator: out = max(0, rear - max(sideL, sideR)).
# Digital inputs are driven by a redstone_block placed ONE BLOCK ABOVE an input dust
# cell (y+1) -> that dust reads 15 (ON); remove the block -> 0 (OFF). Those toggler
# cells are placed by the test driver, NOT here. y=100 stone = support, y=101 = logic.
#
# Gate outputs are dust/comparator cells the difftest validates directly (NO lamps,
# to avoid the known wire-end-adjacent-lamp compiler divergence).

# ---------------- NOT  (lane Z=10)  out=15-A ----------------
fill 8 100 10 13 100 12 minecraft:stone
fill 8 101 10 13 102 12 minecraft:air
setblock 9 101 10 minecraft:redstone_block
setblock 10 101 10 minecraft:comparator[facing=west,mode=subtract]
setblock 11 101 10 minecraft:redstone_wire
setblock 12 101 10 minecraft:redstone_wire
setblock 10 101 11 minecraft:redstone_wire

# ---------------- OR  (lane Z=20)  out=A|B (repeater-cleaned) ----------------

setblock 10 101 21 minecraft:redstone_wire
setblock 11 101 20 minecraft:redstone_wire
setblock 11 101 21 minecraft:redstone_wire
setblock 12 101 20 minecraft:repeater[facing=west]
setblock 13 101 20 minecraft:redstone_wire
